Server Services

Our servers are trained professionals who are experienced in providing top-notch customer service. They are knowledgeable about menu items, wine and cocktail pairings, and can handle any special requests or dietary restrictions. They will ensure that your guests feel welcome and taken care of throughout the event. Our servers can assist with everything from passing hors d'oeuvres to clearing tables and managing the flow of the event.

Bartender Services

Our bartenders are skilled mixologists who can create delicious cocktails and drinks that will impress your guests. They are knowledgeable about different types of alcohol and can provide recommendations for drinks that pair well with the menu. They are also experts in managing the bar area and ensuring that drinks are served quickly and efficiently. Our bartenders are licensed and insured, so you can be confident that you are getting a qualified professional.

Kitchen Assistant

Our kitchen assistants are experienced in all aspects of food preparation and can help keep your kitchen running smoothly. They can assist with tasks such as food prep, cooking, plating, and cleaning up. They are knowledgeable about food safety and sanitation, and can ensure that your kitchen meets all health and safety standards. Our kitchen assistants are trained to work quickly and efficiently, so you can focus on other aspects of your event or business.
