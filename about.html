About the Founder

Hi, my name is Carla and I love hosting. Whether its a dinner party, birthday party, or no reason at all, theres nothing I love more than bringing friends and family together while making sure every detail is on point. From putting together a beautiful table to making sure guests glasses are full, it’s no easy task and even more difficult to handle on your own. There is a downside to hosting: not being able to truly enjoy yourself to the fullest because of everything that needs to be done before, during and after guests leave.

It’s ideal to hire someone to make the hosting experience easier, but it’s difficult to find trustworthy and trained staff. Step in Hostly, the platform that will be the hero for all your hosting needs. The convenience of hiring staff in advance or last minute is something that is appreciated in our on-demand lifestyles. And who wants to go through the effort of hosting to not be able to enjoy themselves? Let our team do the work for you so you can sit back, relax, finish that glass of wine and not miss out on the good conversation.
